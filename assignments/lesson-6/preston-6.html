Elementary school kids live their dream!

By Jane Spears

Preston Elementary School Students were clear to go for their hiking field trip today! Kids have been excited for this trip since kindergarden! The weather has been changing too much lately for them to know if it would have been cancelled or not. With the cold snow in their way, playing in Zion's Park seemed impossible. The sun cleared a few days ago and had been melting the snow until tuesday night that the snow came back. The school was about to cancel the field trip. Fortunately, with WEATHERNOW they were able to get the info right away for today's weather and realized that it was going to be a beautiful day! Enjoy this memorable sunny bright day kids!
