Welcome to Temple Inn-Suites

Welcome to the Temple-Inn Suites Home Page. We're a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. We cater to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. Our overall goal is to provide information about the specialized services that this hotel chain offers to meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips. You can visit our Reservations Page to reserve your suite, medium, and or 2 bed rooms. We are also glad to tell you about the Temple Activities you can perform with a large group of friends, youth and family. We hope you enjoy your stay with us! To Make a Reservation Click Here!
